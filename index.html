Protected Areas

Protected areas, like national parks and wildlife reserves,are essential for conserving biodiversity and protecting ecosystems from human activities.These areas help preserve endangered species and maintain ecological balance,which is increasingly important amid threats like deforestation and climate change.Additionally, protected areas support scientific research, environmental education,and sustainable tourism, emphasizing their crucial role in promoting global ecological sustainability.
